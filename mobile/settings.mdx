# Settings

Manage your account and app preferences on mobile.

## Account settings

### Profile

Update your profile:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Tap your profile picture to change it
3. Update your display name
4. Tap **Save**

### Change password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Tap **Change Password**
3. Enter current and new password
4. Tap **Update**

## App preferences

### Theme

1. Go to **Settings** → **Appearance**
2. Choose:
   - **Light**
   - **Dark**
   - **System** (follows device settings)

### Notifications

1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Configure notification preferences
3. Set quiet hours

## Biometric authentication

Enable Face ID or fingerprint:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Enable **Biometric Authentication**
3. Follow the prompts to set up

## Data and storage

### Clear cache

1. Go to **Settings** → **Data**
2. Tap **Clear Cache**
3. Confirm

### Offline mode

Activities are cached for offline access. Changes sync when you're back online.

## Privacy

### Blocked users

1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Tap **Blocked Users**
3. Tap **Unblock** to remove a block

## Sign out

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Sign Out**
