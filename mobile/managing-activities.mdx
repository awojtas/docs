# Managing activities

Manage your Turnsies activities on iOS and Android.

## Editing an activity

1. Tap on an activity to open it
2. Tap the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
3. Update:
   - Activity name
   - Participants
   - Activity image
   - Rotation mode
4. Tap **Save**

## Rotation modes

- **Sequential**: Participants take turns in order
- **Random**: Next participant is selected randomly
- **Manual**: You choose who goes next

## Archiving activities

1. Open an activity
2. Tap the menu icon (three dots)
3. Tap **Archive**
4. Confirm

Access archived activities from the menu.

## Turn history

1. Open an activity
2. Tap **History**
3. View all completed turns with timestamps

## Filtering activities

On the home screen:

- Tap the filter icon
- Choose **Active**, **Archived**, or **All**
- Use the search bar to find activities

## Deleting an activity

<Warning>
Deleting an activity is permanent.
</Warning>

1. Open an activity
2. Tap the menu icon
3. Tap **Delete**
4. Confirm the deletion
