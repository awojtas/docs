# Contact us

We're here to help with any questions or issues.

## Email support

Send us an email at **support@turnsies.app**

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

## What to include

When contacting support, please include:

- **Description**: Clear description of your issue or question
- **Steps to reproduce**: If reporting a bug, steps to reproduce it
- **Device/browser**: What device and browser/app version you're using
- **Screenshots**: If applicable, screenshots help us understand the issue

## Bug reports

Found a bug? Please report it with:

1. What you were trying to do
2. What happened instead
3. Steps to reproduce the issue
4. Device and browser/app version
5. Screenshots or screen recording (if possible)

## Feature requests

Have an idea for a new feature? We'd love to hear it!

Email us with:
- Description of the feature
- How it would help you
- Any examples from other apps

## Social media

Follow us for updates:

- **Twitter**: [@turnsiesapp](https://twitter.com/turnsiesapp)
- **GitHub**: [github.com/awojtas/turnsies](https://github.com/awojtas/turnsies)

## Response time

- **Email**: 24-48 hours
- **Bug reports**: We prioritize critical bugs and aim to respond within 24 hours
- **Feature requests**: We review all requests but may not respond individually

Thank you for using Turnsies!
