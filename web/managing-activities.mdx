# Managing activities

Learn how to manage your Turnsies activities effectively.

## Editing an activity

1. Click on an activity to open its details
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
3. Update any of the following:
   - Activity name
   - Participants (add or remove)
   - Activity image
   - Rotation mode
4. Click **Save** to apply changes

## Rotation modes

Turnsies supports different rotation modes:

- **Sequential**: Participants take turns in order
- **Random**: Next participant is selected randomly
- **Manual**: You choose who goes next

To change rotation mode:
1. Edit the activity
2. Select **Rotation Mode**
3. Choose your preferred mode

## Archiving activities

Archive activities you no longer need:

1. Open the activity
2. Click **Archive** in the menu
3. Confirm the action

Archived activities are moved to the Archive section and can be restored later.

## Viewing turn history

See who completed turns and when:

1. Open an activity
2. Click **Turn History**
3. View the complete history with timestamps

## Filtering and sorting

On the home screen:

- **Filter**: Show active, archived, or all activities
- **Sort**: By name, last turn, or creation date
- **Search**: Find activities by name

## Deleting an activity

<Warning>
Deleting an activity is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Warning>

1. Open the activity
2. Click **Delete** in the menu
3. Confirm the deletion
