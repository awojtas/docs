# Settings

Manage your account settings and preferences.

## Account settings

### Profile information

Update your profile:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Profile**
3. Update your:
   - Display name
   - Email address
   - Profile picture
4. Click **Save**

### Change password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click **Update Password**

### Email verification

If your email is not verified:

1. Check your inbox for the verification email
2. Click the verification link
3. Or go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Resend Verification Email**

## Preferences

### Theme

Choose between light and dark mode:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Appearance**
2. Select **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** (follows your device)

### Notifications

Configure email notifications:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Toggle notifications for:
   - Turn reminders
   - Invitation received
   - Activity updates
3. Click **Save**

## Privacy

### Blocked users

View and manage blocked users:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Click **Blocked Users**
3. Click **Unblock** to remove a block

### Delete account

<Warning>
Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your activities and data will be deleted.
</Warning>

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Click **Delete My Account**

## Data export

Export your data:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Data**
2. Click **Export Data**
3. You'll receive an email with a download link

The export includes:
- All activities
- Turn history
- Participant information
