# Mobile app quickstart

<Note>
The Turnsies mobile app is in development. iOS and Android apps coming soon.
</Note>

## What to expect

The mobile app will sync with the web version. Create activities on your phone, complete turns on the go, and see changes instantly across all devices.

## Current availability

Use [turnsies.app](https://turnsies.app) on your mobile browser. It works on any device and syncs in real-time.
