# Mobile app quickstart

Get up and running with the Turnsies mobile app.

## Download the app

<Note>
The Turnsies mobile app is currently in development. Check back soon for download links.
</Note>

- **iOS**: Coming soon to the App Store
- **Android**: Coming soon to Google Play

## Create an account

1. Open the Turnsies app
2. Tap **Sign up**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Verify your email address
5. Sign in with your credentials

You can also sign in with Google.

## Create your first activity

1. Tap the **+** button on the home screen
2. Enter an activity name
3. Add participants
4. Choose an activity image (optional)
5. Tap **Create**

## Track a turn

1. Find your activity on the home screen
2. Tap **Complete Turn** when someone finishes
3. The next person is automatically selected
4. View turn history by tapping the activity

## Sync with web

Your Turnsies account syncs automatically between mobile and web:

- Activities created on mobile appear on web
- Changes made on web sync to mobile
- Turn history is always up to date
