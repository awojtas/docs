# Inviting users

Invite other Turnsies users to collaborate on activities.

## How invitations work

When you invite someone:
1. They receive an email invitation
2. They can accept or decline
3. Once accepted, they can view and update the activity
4. Changes sync in real-time for all participants

## Sending an invitation

1. Open an activity
2. Click **Invite Participant**
3. Enter the user's email address
4. Click **Send Invitation**

The user will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation.

## Managing invitations

### View pending invitations

1. Open an activity
2. Click **Participants**
3. See pending invitations with a "Pending" badge

### Cancel an invitation

1. Open an activity
2. Click **Participants**
3. Find the pending invitation
4. Click **Cancel Invitation**

### Resend an invitation

If someone didn't receive the invitation:

1. Cancel the existing invitation
2. Send a new invitation to the same email

## Accepting invitations

When you receive an invitation:

1. Click the link in the email
2. Sign in to your Turnsies account (or create one)
3. Review the activity details
4. Click **Accept** or **Decline**

Accepted activities appear on your home screen.

## Permissions

All participants in an activity can:
- View the activity and turn history
- Complete turns
- Add or remove participants
- Edit activity details
- Archive or delete the activity

<Note>
There is currently no role-based permission system. All participants have equal access.
</Note>

## Blocking users

If you receive unwanted invitations:

1. Open the invitation
2. Click **Block Sender**
3. You won't receive future invitations from this user

To unblock:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Blocked Users**
3. Click **Unblock** next to the user
