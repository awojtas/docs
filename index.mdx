# Welcome to Turnsies

Track turns with your partner, friends, and colleagues. Never wonder "who paid last?" or "whose turn is it?" again.

## What is Turnsies?

Turnsies is a turn-tracking application that helps you manage shared responsibilities and activities. Whether it's household chores, paying for coffee, or any recurring activity, Turnsies keeps everyone on the same page.

## Key features

- **Turn tracking**: Keep track of whose turn it is for any activity
- **Multiple participants**: Add friends, family, or colleagues to your activities
- **Activity history**: See who did what and when
- **Flexible rotation**: Support for different rotation modes (sequential, random, etc.)
- **Cross-platform**: Available on web and mobile (iOS/Android)
- **Real-time sync**: Changes sync instantly across all devices
