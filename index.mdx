# Welcome to Turnsies

Track turns with your partner, friends, and colleagues. Never wonder "who paid last?" or "whose turn is it?" again.

## What is Turnsies?

Turnsies is a turn-tracking application that helps you manage shared responsibilities and activities. Whether it's household chores, paying for coffee, or any recurring activity, Turnsies keeps everyone on the same page.

## Key features

- **Turn tracking**: Keep track of whose turn it is for any activity
- **Multiple participants**: Add friends, family, or colleagues to your activities
- **Activity history**: Complete turn history with timestamps
- **Automatic rotation**: Schedule turns to rotate automatically (daily, weekly, fortnightly, or monthly)
- **Rewards system**: Track money or points earned for completing turns
- **Participant groups**: Create reusable groups for faster activity setup
- **Email invitations**: Invite registered users to collaborate on activities
- **Due dates**: Set recurring due dates for activities
- **Skip rules**: Configure who can skip turns (everyone, own turns only, or no skips)
- **Admin permissions**: Designate admins who can manage activities
- **Cross-platform**: Available on web and mobile (iOS/Android coming soon)
- **Real-time sync**: Changes sync instantly across all devices
